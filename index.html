Brief History

Napoleon Bonaparte was a military leader who ruled over the French Empire. He conquered a large portion of Europe during his reign spanning from Portugal all the way up to Russia. Napoleon was a strategic genius with some of his innovative methods he used to organise his army still being used to this day.


During the French revolution Napoleon rose through the Military ranks and quickly seized control over France claiming power through a coup d’état. Once he had control over France he went on a long campaign claiming a lot of terrortory in Europe and developed what was known as the 'La Grande Armée' which became an extremely feared army that would go on to win many battles with excellent strategy.
