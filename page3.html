Personal History

Here are some facts about Napoleons personal life.

Upbringing

  • Born on the 15th of August 1769 in Ajaccio, Corisca
  • Carlo Buonaparte was his father and he was a lawyer married to his mother named Letizia Ramolino.
  • He had 7 brothers and sisters.
  • He moved to France and started his education there from the age of 9.
  • Struggled with feeling like a foreigner as he did not hold the same traditions and didn't speak the same language.
  • Spent 5 years at the military college of Brienne.
  • He then spent one year at the military academy in Paris.
  • Once he finished military training he became second lieutenant of arillery.

General Facts

  • Napoleon was 5ft 5in tall (1.69m).
  • He wrote a romance novel named 'Clisson et Eugénie"
  • Was married to 2 women during his reign.
  • First wife was called Désirée Clary, the Queen of Sweden.
  • Second wife was called Joséphine de Beauharnais.
  • He used to dress himself as a lower class citizen and walk around Paris asking questions about what they thought of him as Emperor.
  • Attached to his neck at all times was a vial of poison in case he ever was captured he could drink it can kill himself.
  • Napoleon possibly had a fear of cats.
